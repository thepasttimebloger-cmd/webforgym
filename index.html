About Us

Not just a gym, a Community Staple

While JR Fitness 24x7 doesn't have a grand, centuries-old historical background like a monument, it possesses a solid local history as one of the staple fitness centers in the Vrindavan Colony & Vrindavan Yojana area of Lucknow.

Origins & Establishment

Timeline JR Fitness has been operating in the Vrindavan Colony area for roughly 7 to 9 years (established around 2015–2017).
Location Anchored in a prime spot—Plot No. 3M-08, Sector 3, opposite SKD Academy on Raebareli Road. This strategic location attracts a steady stream of locals, students, and professionals in the rapidly developing Vrindavan Yojana.
